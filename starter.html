Welcome to Assetra!

Explore our all-in-one portal for lifting sling inspections, damage reporting, compliance tracking, and more — discover the powerful features below.

New Inspection ▼

Start a new inspection by selecting the inspection type: Initial, Frequent, or Periodic. Complete the required fields, upload supporting images, and document compliance according to applicable standards. Bulk upload options are available for adding multiple new slings simultaneously.

Update Inspection ▼

Edit existing inspection records flagged as Warning or Fail. Update status after repairs, upload new test certificates, and adjust damage findings as necessary. Manually reclassify equipment as Pass, Warning, or Fail based on the latest condition assessment.

View Records ▼

Search and review a complete history of all inspections performed. Filter by status, date range, asset ID, or inspection type. View detailed records, supporting images, and certificate uploads associated with each item.

Download Reports ▼

Generate formal inspection reports in PDF format for individual assets or bulk asset groups. Export full compliance histories, including images, notes, status changes, and supporting documentation, for internal audits or regulatory review.

Account Settings ▼

Manage personal and organizational account details, including login credentials, permission levels, notification settings, and data export preferences. Configure multi-user access if enabled.

Help Center ▼

Access step-by-step user guides, regulatory references, troubleshooting FAQs, and direct support contact options. Find information on inspection criteria, system usage, and equipment compliance standards.
